## What is a nonce?

The term 'nonce' is a [portmanteau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portmanteau) of '**n**umber used **on**ly on**ce**': every single transaction you _send_ from your MetaMask wallet has one, and the number (starting with 0 for the first transaction you send) increases in increments of 1.

Each transaction's nonce is an identifier that ensures your transactions are processed by the network in the correct order, preventing ['double spend' scenarios](https://support.mycrypto.com/general-knowledge/ethereum-blockchain/what-is-nonce/).

In MetaMask, adjusting the nonce can be useful if you need to [speed up or cancel pending transactions](/transactions-and-gas/transactions/how-to-speed-up-or-cancel-a-pending-transaction).

## How do I customize the nonce when sending a transaction?

<Tabs>

<TabItem value="Extension" label="Extension">

Customizing the nonce isn't possible unless you enable it. To do so, head to settings by clicking the three vertical dots in the top right of your wallet, then go to Settings > Advanced:

<div class="center-container">
  <div class="img-small">
    <img

    />
  </div>
</div>

Start to [send a transaction](/managing-my-tokens/moving-your-tokens/how-to-send-tokens-from-your-metamask-wallet) from your wallet. Enter the recipient address and the value, and click 'Next'. Before you confirm the transaction, you'll see a breakdown of the gas fees and other details that looks like this:

<div class="center-container">
  <div class="img-small">
    <img
      src={
        require("../../../img/transactions-and-gas/transactions/how-to-customize-a-transaction-nonce/MetaMask_customize_transaction_nonce_transaction.png")
          .default
      }
      alt="MetaMask_customize_transaction_nonce_transaction.png"
    />
  </div>
</div>

Click the 'Custom nonce' field to enter the number you need, and, when you're ready to submit the transaction, 'Confirm'.

</TabItem>
