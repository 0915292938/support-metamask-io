When you download the browser extension from MetaMask's [official website](https://metamask.io/download.html), please check your browser's extensions. Depending on the browser you are on, the steps are as follows:

<Tabs>

<TabItem value="Chrome" label="Chrome">

You will see your extensions under this icon. Clicking on it will bring down the dropdown where you can pin your MetaMask extension:

<div class="center-container">
  <div class="img-large">
    <img
      src={
        require("../../../img/managing-my-wallet/using-metamask/how-to-find-and-manage-your-metamask-extension-on-chrome-and-firefox/Manage_MetaMask_Extension_Chrome2.1.gif")
          .default
      }
      alt="Manage MetaMask Extension Chrome"
    />
  </div>
</div>

You can find details about your extension in the _Manage Extensions_ section: chrome://extensions/ or by clicking the 3 dots menu on the right next to the extension you would like to manage.

</TabItem>

<TabItem value="Firefox" label="Firefox">

Click this menu in the browser where you will see **Add-ons and Themes,** or navigate to **about:addons.**

<div class="center-container">
  <div class="img-large">
    <img
      src={
        require("../../../img/managing-my-wallet/using-metamask/how-to-find-and-manage-your-metamask-extension-on-chrome-and-firefox/Manage_MetaMask_Extension_Firefox1.1.gif")
          .default
      }
      alt="Manage MetaMask Extension Firefox"
    />
  </div>
</div>

On the next screen, click on 'Extensions' on the left-hand side:

<div class="center-container">
  <div class="img-large">
    <img
      src={
        require("../../../img/managing-my-wallet/using-metamask/how-to-find-and-manage-your-metamask-extension-on-chrome-and-firefox/Manage_MetaMask_Extension_Firefox2.1.gif")
          .default
      }
      alt="Manage MetaMask Extension Firefox"
    />
  </div>
</div>

To make your extension visible on Firefox, enable it by switching the tab slider.

</TabItem>

</Tabs>
